Enchanting Elixirs and Eats

~~INTRODUCTION~~

This project was a project for us to complete since we just finished learning about wireframes and interactivity. We could choose between a few topics but I chose Food & Beverage because I'm most familiar with that category. Then I had the idea of like a fantasy cafe or tavern, based on those themed cafes everywhere, I've even visited the Harry Potter themed cafe before and it was really cool. When thinking of the details, I was imagining myself in the store runnning the place like a cafe/club/tavern and serving food and drinks to all kinds of fantasy creatures and people, which is kind of the whole idea of the cafe that I had.

~~DESIGN~~

DESIGN IDEAS

For this design, I wanted to use like a blue-ish purple colour for the background as a mix of blue and purple, my 2 favourite colours. But I felt it was a bit too bright for the menu items to stand out so I used that colour for the background of the menu item card and used a much darker shade for the main background, then I used a shade somewhere in the middle for the navbar. For the fonts, I picked those that look more "Fantasy" like a, you might find this kind of handwriting in a letter from a fantasy land, kind of handwriting.

PAGES

~~OVERALL~~

For a first high-fi wireframe with interactivity, I would say it looks really good. It gave me the opportunity to try implementing proper interactivity like how a normal website would work along with teaching how to use conditional variables like for the "Add to Cart" changing to "Out of Stock" only when it becomes 0 left. Overall I'm quite happy with the wireframe and the interactivity.
