StyleShop Wireframe

~~INTRODUCTION~~

This project was a CA project about a user-friendly shopping site called StyleShop. It includes a UI Kit and a wireframe with interactability. We could sell whatever clothes items we wanted and design based on that. So I picked selling cute hoodies because the day before my mom was like showing me cute hoodies on TaoBao so it kinda just stuck with me for the project.

~~DESIGN~~

CUTE DESIGN

For the design, I based mine partially on what I wanted to sell and the colours I wanted to use, I wanted to use like a light-blue range because my favourite colour is blue. When paired with white it looked like the sky and giving off a dreamy feeling. So I decided to "sell" the cute hoodies to match the aesthetic of the site.

PAGES

~~OVERALL~~

I like this app alot, the style of the platform feels very accurate to the type of product being sold and the customers coming to buy these items with the whole Dreamy Theme. It was very rushed with no sketches or low-fidelity wireframe due to time contraints and it not being a requirement for the CA, I would say it came out really nicely.
